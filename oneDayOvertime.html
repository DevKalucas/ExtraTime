Single Day Calculator

With this calculator you are able to calculate your overtime for a single day.
Enter your arrival time, your break time and lastly your overtime.

How does the calculation work?

The calculation is based on the following formula:
Arrival Time + Break Time + Overtime + 7.5 hours = Departure Time

FAQ

Q: How much overtime is allowed?

A: The law states that you are only allowed to work 60h a week.

Q: Are there taxes on my overtime payment?

A: Yes, there are! The same taxes from your normal pay apply to your overtime pay.
